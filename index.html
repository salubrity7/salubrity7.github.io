Our Departments

Medical coding departments within healthcare organizations play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and compliant coding of medical procedures, diagnoses, and services provided to patients.

Inpatient Coding Department

Responsible for coding diagnoses, procedures, and treatments performed during the inpatient stay.

Outpatient Coding Department

Specializes in coding for surgical procedures, ensuring that each procedure is accurately reflected with the appropriate codes for reimbursement.

Emergency Department Coding Department

Codes services provided in emergency care settings, including treatments, diagnostic tests, and emergency surgeries.

Radiology Coding Department

Focuses on coding for diagnostic imaging procedures such as X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, ultrasounds, and other radiological services.

Pediatrics Coding Department

Specializes in coding for pediatric services, which may involve unique codes for childhood diseases, vaccinations, and pediatric procedures.

Cardiology Coding Department

Specializes in coding for cardiology services, including diagnostic tests, heart surgeries, and other cardiology-related treatments.

Coding Compliance & Auditing Department

Ensures that the entire coding process follows legal, regulatory, and payer-specific guidelines.

Medical Billing & Coding Department

This department bridges the gap between coding and financial operations.

Long-Term Care Coding Department

Specializes in coding for services provided in long-term care facilities, such as nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, and hospice care.
